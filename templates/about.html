Long Description

This website is created with the purpose of assisting IDEA members. Please be aware of the fact that this website is only for viewing schedule of unions in Tokyo. This cannot make reservation, nor anything else.

Display Logic

  • Similar events are putting together as one event, based on a simple string comparison.
    As a result, a event may appear as multiple events due to discrepancies in writing of different unions.
  • Can perform search, in other words, filter out results.
  • The schedule is refreshed every 6 hours.
  • "Schedule of Next Month" will be under the tab of "Schedule of This Month" on the first day of each month.
  • "Schedule of Next Month" will only be available when it is uploaded on COM page of IDEA.
